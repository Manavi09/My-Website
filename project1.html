Stock Pricing Analysis using SAS

Tools: SAS, Statistical Modeling
Duration: 3 months
Type: Financial Analysis

Project Overview

This project involved comprehensive analysis of stock market trends using advanced statistical methods and time series forecasting to predict price movements and identify investment opportunities.

Key Achievements

  • Developed predictive models with 85% accuracy for short-term price movements
  • Analyzed 10+ years of historical stock data across multiple sectors
  • Created automated reporting dashboards for real-time insights
  • Identified key market indicators affecting stock performance

Methodology

The analysis utilized advanced SAS procedures including PROC ARIMA for time series modeling, PROC REG for regression analysis, and PROC FORECAST for predictive modeling. Data preprocessing involved handling missing values, outlier detection, and feature engineering.

Results & Impact

The model successfully predicted market trends with high accuracy, enabling better investment decision-making and risk management strategies.
