About Me

Naturally curious and passionate Data Analyst dedicated to transforming raw data into actionable insights. With a solid foundation in Data Analytics and Business Intelligence, I excel at analyzing complex datasets and helping businesses make informed decisions.

My Professional Journey
My journey into data analysis commenced with a degree in Computer Science Engineering at Nagpur University, India. I had several database foundation courses during my bachelors. I have always been fascinated by the fact that we have so much to learn in the world and data being the source of it. This curiosity led me to pursue a Masters degree in Business Analytics from The University of Texas at Dallas. After graduation, I have been working with major consulting companies like Atos Syntel and HCLTech for more than 4.5 years with clients in Banking, Financial Services, Transportation and Logistics. Over the course of my career, I've refined my proficiency in SQL, Tableau, Python, SAS and Excel, tackling diverse data challenges efficiently.

On a More Personal Perspective
Beyond my professional pursuits, I'm also a trained Kathak dancer, a fashion model, and enjoy playing musical instruments like the Synthesizer and Ukulele. This creative angle helps me bring fresh perspectives to every data project I undertake.
