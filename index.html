About Me

Naturally curious and passionate Data Analyst dedicated to transforming raw data into actionable insights. With a solid foundation in Data Analytics and Business Intelligence, I excel at analyzing complex datasets and helping businesses make informed decisions.

My Professional Journey​​ My journey into data analysis commenced with a degree in Computer Science Engineering at Nagpur University, India. I had several database foundation courses during my bachelors. I have always been fascinated by the fact that we have so much to learn in the world and data being the source of it. Be it everyday activities like learning a new language, exploring travel destinations, deciding what to order from your favorite restaurant or be it transforming businesses to make critical decisions that define sustainable growth while ensuring customer satisfaction, Data has a role to play everywhere. This curiosity led me to pursue a Masters degree in Business Analytics from The University of Texas at Dallas. After Graduation, I have been working with major Consulting companies like Atos Syntel and HCLTech for more than 4.5 years with clients in Banking, Financial Services, Transportation and Logistics. Over the course of my career, I've refined my proficiency in a multitude of analytical tools and programming languages, including SQL, Tableau, Python, SAS and Excel, allowing me to tackle diverse data challenges efficiently.
