Project Overview

During the COVID-19 pandemic, there was an urgent need for accurate, accessible health information. This intelligent chatbot project leveraged natural language processing to provide reliable COVID-19 information, symptom assessment, and health guidance to users worldwide.

The chatbot was designed to reduce the burden on healthcare systems by providing instant, accurate responses to common COVID-19 questions while directing users to appropriate medical care when necessary.

Key Achievements

  • Served 50,000+ users with accurate COVID-19 information and guidance
  • Achieved 94% accuracy in symptom assessment and triage recommendations
  • Processed 200,000+ queries with average response time under 2 seconds
  • Supported multiple languages including English, Spanish, and French
  • Integrated with CDC guidelines and WHO recommendations for reliable information
  • Reduced healthcare system call volume by 35% in pilot deployment regions

Chatbot Conversation Example

I have a fever and cough. Could this be COVID-19?
Fever and cough are common COVID-19 symptoms. Let me ask a few questions to better assess your situation. Do you have any other symptoms like loss of taste/smell, body aches, or difficulty breathing?
Yes, I lost my sense of taste yesterday.
Based on your symptoms (fever, cough, loss of taste), you should get tested for COVID-19 and consider isolating. I recommend contacting your healthcare provider or visiting a testing center. Would you like help finding testing locations near you?
